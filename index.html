Mantax Blue Caliper

Description

Mantax Blue is available in the largest selection of scale lengths, graduation options and models. This caliper model has detachable, adjustable and exchangeable jaws, non-wear scale print, and a durable and ergonomic design.


Variety of Aplications

Mantax Blue calipers are used in a wide variety of applications and many different operation areas such as diameter measuring of trees and timber, log scaling, in fishery industry & wildlife management, in contruction and in healthcare industry.
